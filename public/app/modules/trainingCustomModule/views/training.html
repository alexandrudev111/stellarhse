Instructions

  • 1. Scroll to the custom fields area at the bottom of the page.
  • 2. Drag and drop custom fields into the highlighted area.
  • 3. Click on the field title to open the text editor.
  • 4. Save your changes to close the text editor.
  • 5. Reorganize your new fields by dragging and dropping them.
  • 6. Save your changes and open the form in the HSE Tracking module to verify.
